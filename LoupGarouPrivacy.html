2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to maintain, analyze, and improve the app. The categories of data we may process are described below.

2.1 Local Game Data (Stays on Your Device)

To set up a game, players enter first names or nicknames so that the app can assign roles around the table.

Data processed may include:

  • first names or nicknames entered for a game;
  • game settings and role composition;
  • onboarding and in-app tips display status.

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or to any third party. It is erased by deleting the application. Players may enter fictional nicknames instead of real names at any time.

Purpose: running a game around the table and remembering the player list for the next game night.

Legal basis: performance of the contract.

2.2 Analytics Data

We use the Amplitude SDK to understand how users interact with the app and to improve the product experience.

Data processed may include:

  • a pseudonymous device or install identifier;
  • usage events, such as a game being started or finished, the number of players, the roles selected, the winning team, or the onboarding being completed;
  • app version;
  • device type;
  • operating system;
  • language and region settings;
  • technical event timestamps.

We do not send player names or nicknames to Amplitude. Analytics events contain only aggregate game information, never the identity of the people sitting around the table.

We use analytics data only for internal product analytics, such as understanding which roles and options are popular, identifying friction points, improving onboarding, improving retention, and making the app more useful and reliable.

We do not use Amplitude analytics data for third-party advertising purposes.

Purpose: product analytics, app improvement, performance analysis, and internal statistics.

Legal basis: legitimate interest for privacy-friendly internal analytics, or consent where required by law or platform rules.

2.3 Waitlist Email (You vs AI Mode)

We are preparing a future You vs AI mode. If, and only if, you choose to join the waitlist for this mode, we collect your email address so we can notify you when it becomes available.

Data processed:

  • the email address you voluntarily submit;
  • your language or region setting, so we can send the notification in the right language;
  • the date on which you gave your consent.

We collect only your email address for this purpose. No name and no device identifier is attached to it, and this email is kept separate from the analytics data described above. This data is stored on our own servers located in the European Union.

We use this email only to notify you when the You vs AI mode launches. We never sell it and never use it for any other purpose. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email, or by contacting us at contact@innoway.app to request access, correction, or deletion.

Purpose: notifying you when the You vs AI mode launches.

Legal basis: your consent.

2.4 What We Do Not Collect

  • no account is required to play the game;
  • no email address is collected to play; an email address is collected only if you voluntarily join the You vs AI waitlist (see section 2.3);
  • no phone number;
  • no location data;
  • no contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access;
  • no advertising identifier (IDFA) and no cross-app tracking;
  • no payment data: the app is free, with no purchases;
  • no chat, no user-generated content leaving the device.

  • Right to deletion: you may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Right to restriction: you may request restriction of certain processing activities.
  • Right to object: you may object to certain processing activities based on legitimate interest.
