About Us

At InnoWay, we believe the future is AI seamlessly integrated into everyday experiences. Our expertise lies in mobile app development powered by artificial intelligence, where we focus on creating tools that feel intuitive and effortless for users. We aim for AI to work silently in the background, enhancing functionality without requiring users to directly engage with it.

In addition to development, we specialize in mobile growth and user acquisition, with a strong focus on ASO and performance marketing. We've mastered TikTok as a global acquisition channel, generating over 5 million views across campaigns and successfully scaling user bases in multiple countries.

Our Flashcards AI App, is an intelligent learning tool that has been downloaded more than 150,000 times, with a peak daily usage of 1,500+ active users. It reflects our core mission: using AI to create smart, frictionless products that improve how people learn and grow.
